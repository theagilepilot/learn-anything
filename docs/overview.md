# Application Overview

This application helps learners generate personalized learning plans, lessons, and quizzes from a topic prompt. The core goal is to adapt content to a preferred learning style while tracking progress in a structured dashboard.

## What it does

- Generates a multi-module syllabus based on a topic and timeframe.
- Produces detailed lessons for each module, tailored to a learning style.
- Creates quizzes for knowledge checks and shows results.
- Tracks progress through modules and lessons.

## Intended audience

- Self-directed learners who want structured guidance.
- Educators or coaches creating fast, tailored learning paths.
- Anyone who benefits from learning-style adaptation.

## Learning styles

The app supports four learning styles:

- Visual: Diagram-heavy explanations and visual metaphors.
- Mixed: Balanced text, visuals, and exercises.
- Reading/Writing: Text-focused explanations and written summaries.
- Hands-on: Practical examples and mini-projects.

## Key principles

- Personalization: Content adapts to learner preferences.
- Clarity: Lessons emphasize structure and practical understanding.
- Momentum: Short feedback loops through quizzes and progress tracking.
